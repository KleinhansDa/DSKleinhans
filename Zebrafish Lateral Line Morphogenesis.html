Summary & Outlook

Summary

  1. More Cell Clusters deposited in shroom3 mutants
    • merging events
    • splitting events
  2. Neuromasts are morphologically similar to the wildtype
  3. Proliferation probably not increased in the pLLP
    • Cell clusters may compensate low cell numbers by proliferation / regeneration
  4. shroom3 mutants differ more more in rosettiness than in number of rosettes
  5. Unclear wheater there's a morpholical feedback in hair cell specification
  6. Rosette formation is impaired in shroom3 mutants
    • more cells are oriented along the A-P axis
    • AC along Major is more affected than along Minor

Outlook

  1. Correlate speed of migration with deposition frequency
  2. Back up Proliferation data
    • EdU / Phosphohistone for the pLLP
    • Cell cluster proliferation
    • Cell cluster regeneration
  3. Look at haircell specification in timelapse movies

Future Perspectives

  1. Technical

  2. Functional
